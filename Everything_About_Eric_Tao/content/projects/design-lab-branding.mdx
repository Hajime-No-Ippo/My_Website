## Concept
Built a monogram that blends precision with creative energy, with a flexible grid for print and digital.

## Process
- Moodboard and type exploration to match tone.
- Iterated logo marks in Illustrator; refined spacing and optical balance.
- Defined color palette and typography pairings; delivered usage guidelines.

## Deliverables
- Primary/secondary logos and lockups.
- Color and typography specs.
- Social and presentation templates.
